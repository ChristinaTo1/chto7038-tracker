Recipe Title
Dessert
Nutritional Value
  • Per Serving (1 muffin):
  • Carbohydrates: 38 g
  • Calories: 232 kcal
  • Protein: 4g
  • Fat 8 g
  • Sodium: 233 mg
Ingredients
  • 2-3 bananas
  • 1 cup of plain yogurt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 4 Tbsp of melted butter
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 cup of chocolate chips
Notes
A nice on-the-go treat that pairs well with coffee!
  • 1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Line the muffin tin with paper liners.
  • 2. Mash the bananas. Substitute with yogurt if there is not enough banana
  • 3. Add mashed banana to a mixing bowl. Add yogurt, eggs, sugar, melted butter an vanilla
